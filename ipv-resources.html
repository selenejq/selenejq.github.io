COMPUTER SECURITY & PRIVACY

for Survivors of Intimate Partner Violence

We have developed a collection of materials, tools, and resources to help IPV survivors, advocates, and technologists discover and mitigate tech-based risks and vulnerabilities. All resources are free to download, use, and modify. We use these materials as part of our Clinic to End Tech Abuse (CETA) for IPV survivors in New York City.

Please visit CETA's resources page to access all available resources: https://www.ceta.tech.cornell.edu/resources
