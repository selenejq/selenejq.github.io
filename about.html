Hey!

Selene is a rising sophomore at Lick-Wilmerding High School in San Francisco, California. She loves participating in (and sometimes winning) hackathons as the design half of a duo with her twin sister, Athena. You can frequently find her working on 3D art in Blender or designing in Figma. She also occasionally dabbles in coding with experience in the languages Python, React, HTML/CSS/SASS, and Swift. Selene loves traveling places, especially to explore and eat. She dreams of backpacking the world with her sisters. She also plays soccer and makes super short doodle animations in Krita in her free time :)
